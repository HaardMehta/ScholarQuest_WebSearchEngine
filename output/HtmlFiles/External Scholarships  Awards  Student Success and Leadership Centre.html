External Scholarships & Awards

The University of Windsor is committed to providing strong student awards and financial aid programs for all students.  This page provides information to undergraduate students about scholarships and awards available from sources external to the university. For information about awards offered by the University of Windsor, please visit the Student Awards page.

To successfully win external awards it is important for you to build a strategy. The first step in any studentâ€™s strategy should be to open an account on websites that offer access to large databases of student awards. We recommend the following:

  • Yconic (formerly Student Awards)
  • Scholarships Canada

Students can also consult the following resources:

  • Student Scholarships
  • Academic Invest
  • ScholarTree

NOTE: The University of Windsor does not promote scholarship opportunities that are offered by corporations. The information on this website is provided for the convenience of students only. Please be aware that scholarship opportunities are increasingly being used to gather email addresses for their commercial value. Please exercise caution in submitting your contact information to anyone in an online setting. If you discover any scholarship links on these pages that appear questionable, please email Tim Brunet (tbrunet@uwindsor.ca). You can learn more about online safety at getcybersafe.ca/en and through this article about scholarship scams.

Further information about external awards is provided via the links below: 

  • Scholarships for Canadian high-school students applying for university admission
  • Local and national scholarships and awards for undergraduate study in Canada
  • International scholarships for Canadians going overseas
  • International scholarships for students from abroad coming to Canada
  • International scholarships for post-graduate study outside Canada

We're always seeking new opportunities for our students. Please send suggestions for additions or changes to Tim Brunet at tbrunet@uwindsor.ca

Good luck with your scholarship search!
