We offer awards and scholarships to energetic and committed computer science students - both undergraduate and graduate - involved with our community here in the Faculty of Computer Science.

Snowball Gala Awards

Ranging from $500 to $3,000, these awards recognize just how important community spirit and social development are to personal and academic growth.

  • The Ada Byron Award
  • The Citizenship Award
  • The Leadership Award

Industry Scholarships

Industry scholarships for both undergraduate and graduate current students:

  • Dash Hudson Scholarship
  • Leacross Foundation Leadership Scholarship
  • MDA Geointelligence Scholarship for Excellence in Computer Science

Industry Bursaries

Industry bursaries for current graduate students:

  • Michael Shepherd and BDANS Bursary in Data Science and Machine Learning
