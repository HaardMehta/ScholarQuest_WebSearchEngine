Transfer Students

University of Windsor Transfer Student Scholarships

If you are a transfer student, please contact our office to determine your eligibility for a transfer scholarship. 

$500 Scholarship

Full-time undergraduate students who are direct transfers from a Canadian or International College or University (excluding Law and Education) with a transfer average between 80% - 84.9% will receive an award valued at $500. Automatic: No application required.

$1000 Scholarship

Full-time undergraduate students who are direct transfers from a Canadian or International College or University (excluding Law and Education) with a transfer average of 85% or greater will receive an award valued at $1000. Automatic: No application required.

Eligibility Criteria

A student transferring from a college or university (domestic or international) who meets the following eligibility criteria will be offered a one-time Transfer Student Scholarship payable upon registration in their first term of full-time study at the University of Windsor:

  • Must be eligible for a minimum of three transfer credits
  • Must have a minimum of an 80% cumulative average (admission average)
  • Must have attended a minimum of 2 semesters of full-time studies in an academic program at a college or university
  • Must have been a registered college/university student for at least one full-time semester in the academic year immediately preceding the start of full-time studies at the University of Windsor.
  • Must be transferring into an undergraduate degree program (with the exception of Law and Education)
Other Considerations
  • Collaborative Nursing students who begin their studies at the college and complete their final two years at the University of Windsor are not eligible.
  • Students completing ELIP as part of their admission requirement can be considered once their ELIP requirements are met (follows the same consideration for entrance scholarship eligibility). 

Learn more how to apply scholarships, awards and bursaries online once you are a registered student at the University of Windsor.

Please ensure that all required supporting documentation is submitted to Student Awards & Financial Aid in a timely manner. Late submission of supporting documentation will result in delays in the application review process.

Book an Appointment

If you feel that you need to talk to someone about your situation, please take the following steps:

1)  Try asking your question using https://ask.uwindsor.ca.  We have many knowledge base articles that might be able to assit you with you question.

2) Log in to https://ask.uwindsor.ca to submit a service request and a Student Awards or Financial Aid Representative will respond to your inquiry as soon as possible.

3) Call our office during our phone service hours.  A Student Award or Financial Aid Representative may be able to resolve your issue over the phone.

4) If options 1) through 3) do not resolve your issue,  we will schedule you a virtual Teams meeting/appointment with a Student Awards or Financial Aid Specialist for a time that is mutually convenient.
