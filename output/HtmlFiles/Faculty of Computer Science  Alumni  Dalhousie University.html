Computer Science

Faculty of Computer Science (FCS) alumni have many reasons to connect and celebrate. Our “We Are All CS” initiative is an ambitious plan to tackle gender diversity, acting as a catalyst for change in breaking down barriers within the sector and faculty.

As a computer science grad, there are many ways for you to get involved with FCS. We organize regular events to bring you together with faculty and students, publish a yearly computer science magazine, and encourage you to share your stories with us and the wider Dal community.

Read news articles featuring the FCS, profile stories of our grads, and alumni-submitted class notes.

Volunteer with us

Whether you’d like to foster connections among alumni in your area, reunite your former classmates back on campus, or engage with alumni worldwide, there’s a volunteer opportunity for you. Let us know how you’d like to help.
