Noteskeyboard_arrow_down

It can seem like an overwhelming challenge to break into a competitive job market after completing your post-secondary education. That’s why the Canadian Marketing Association is stepping in to help you find opportunities, network with peers and industry professionals and get your foot in the door.



CMA NXT is a professional development platform designed to help you navigate the gap between education and employment. Create your free CMA NXT account before September 30, 2023 and get automatically added to a draw for a chance to win $250!

When you join, you will gain instant access to:



  • Insights on what hiring managers are looking for

  • Tips to strengthen and develop your soft skills

  • Guidance on the interview process

  • Access to workshops, events, networking opportunities – and more!




Create a free account today at CMANXT.ca and discover how CMA NXT can help you on your professional journey.
