Scholarships and bursaries

Get the support you need

At Dalhousie you’ll find the funding you need to focus on your studies. Each year, thesis-based students receive more than $24 million in scholarships, stipends and bursaries administered by the university. The amount you will receive varies between degrees and programs, typically ranging from $16,500 to $24,000 or more per year in the early stages of a degree

Find your scholarship

Dalhousie offers or manages a large number of funding opportunities, including those provided by the university, the federal and provincial governments, and external agencies. Explore what is available to you by selecting from the options below.
