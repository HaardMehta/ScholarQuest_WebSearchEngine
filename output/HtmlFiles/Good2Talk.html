Due to maintenance, you may experience service disruptions for Good2Talk Ontario between approximately 9 a.m. ET on July 18 and 10 a.m. ET August 1. During that time, you can call 1-800-668-6868 to speak with a professional counsellor, or text 686868 to connect with a trained, volunteer crisis responder. If you need help immediately, you can contact 911 or the emergency services in your area. We appreciate your patience and understanding.
