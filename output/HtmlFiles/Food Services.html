FOOD SERVICES HOURS

Summer 2023 Hours of Operation

The Market Place and CEI Tim Hortons are open Monday - Friday 8:00 am until 3:00 pm

CAW STUDENT CENTRE

The Market Place is located in the main level of the CAW Student Center.  It is a central hub for retail dining at UWindsor. Market Place features a wide variety of dining options including Booster Juice, Burger Bar, Chef To You, Pasta Bar, PizzaPizza, Subway, and Tim Hortons. Serving soup and salad, sandwiches and wraps, pizza, burritos, baked goods, healthy grab-and-go choices and traditional grill favourites, The MarketPlace is a great campus destination to grab a nutritious meal or a quick snack on campus.

STARBUCKS CAW

Located in the main level of the CAW Student Center, this location is a full-service Starbucks offering a complete selection of coffees, teas and pastries. It also offers a great selection of Starbucks Sandwiches, Grab and Go Choices. A great place to quickly refuel throughout your day.
